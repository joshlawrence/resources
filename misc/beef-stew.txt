# BEEF STEW

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8p-f9DcVkgE

* brown 2 lbs chuck roast cubed (med high heat, 7-10 min), add to pot
* deglaze pan with 1/2 cup of water, add to pot
* cook mirepoix (2:1:1 ratio, onion/celery/carrots) until carrots and onion are caramalized
* add 4 minced garlic cloves, +1 min, add all to pot
* add 2 tsp of butter + mushrooms (brown 4-5 min), add salt + 2 tbl flour +1 min, add to pot

swap to pot

* 1 cup red wine
* 1 cup tomato sauce
  - tomato paste is an option
* few tablespoons of worchestershire
* 1 tsp smoked paprika
* 1/2 tbl sugar
* 2 1/2 tsp of "italian herbs" NEEDS CLARIFICATION
* generous salt
* cracked pepper
* 4 cups beef broth
* stir

cover, bring to a boil, simmer for 1-1 1/2 hours

* 2 cups baby potatos
* 1 cup frozen sweet peas

cook +20 min, finish w/ freshly chopped parsely
