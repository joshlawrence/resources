1 chuck roast about 3 lbs.
1 jar (16oz) whole pepperoncinis (look by the olives)
1 package of dry ranch dressing mix
1 package of Au Jus mix (found by the packaged spices)
3-4 TBSP butter cut up
Sprinkle both seasoning packages on top of the roast
Pour the whole jar of peppers and juice on top of the seasonings
Top with the cut up butter
Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
