# -- AC wiring color codes -- #

black = hot
white = neutral
green = ground

brown = hot
blue = neutral
green/yellow stripe = ground

switch hot
fuse hot

- in a 2-prong cable, ALWAYS used polarized plugs.

indentifying the neutral on 2-prong cables:

white stripe = neutral
ribbed = neutral
white = neutral

WIDE blade is NEUTRAL
SMALL blade is HOT
